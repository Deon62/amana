Better aid is a team effort.

Amana works by connecting everyone in the aid chain — donors, logistics operators, NGOs, and field teams — on a single shared timeline. Our partners make that possible.

Four kinds of partner, one shared mission

Every role in the aid corridor is covered. If you touch the supply chain — upstream or downstream — there is a place for you in the Amana network.

NGOs & Field Partners

Local and international NGOs that run last-mile distribution and community-level verification. You are the most trusted node in the network — Amana puts you in control of the evidence.

Logistics Providers

Freight forwarders, warehouse operators, and transport companies that move goods along aid corridors. Amana integrates with your scan points so you don't carry the reconciliation burden alone.

Technology Partners

Platforms and tooling providers working in the humanitarian space. If your system touches beneficiary data, programme management, or financial flows, we want to build a shared data layer with you.

Institutional Donors

Foundations, government bodies, and multilateral funds that allocate significant resources to humanitarian programming. Amana gives you the audit trail and real-time visibility that programme reports can't provide.

How a partnership begins

We run bounded pilots before any full integration. Here is what the process looks like from first conversation to live corridor.

  1. Tell us about your programme

    Share your organisation, the geography you operate in, and the type of aid corridor you want to digitise.

  2. Map the corridor together

    We facilitate a structured session to trace your Source → Transit → Verification → Delivery flow and identify where Amana can add the most value.

  3. Run a bounded pilot

    We pick one corridor, one programme cycle, and instrument it end-to-end. Both parties review the outcome before deciding whether to expand.

  4. Scale what works

    After a successful pilot, we formalise the partnership and roll the model out to additional corridors and geographies at a pace that suits your organisation.

Ready to build a more accountable aid corridor?

Reach out with your organisation name, the geography you work in, and the kind of programme you want to pilot. We will respond within two business days.
