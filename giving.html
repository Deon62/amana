Your gift, step by step

Traditional giving is a leap of faith. You send money and trust that it eventually reaches the right person. With Amana, that trust is replaced by evidence.

From the moment your contribution is received to the moment a beneficiary confirms delivery, every stage is recorded on a shared, tamper-proof timeline. You get a link — not a newsletter.

  1. 01 You give. Your contribution is logged on-chain with the programme, amount, and intended destination.
  2. 02 Goods move. Smart contracts track the journey through approved warehouses and logistics partners.
  3. 03 Local NGOs verify. Trusted field partners confirm stock and readiness before last-mile distribution.
  4. 04 Beneficiaries confirm. A QR check-in or SMS code closes the loop — your gift is documented end-to-end.
